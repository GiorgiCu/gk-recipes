Description

Start by making the sauce with ground beef, bell peppers, onions, and a combo of tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes.

Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheeses ready. We're using ricotta, shredded mozzarella, and parmesan.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Crushed Tomatos

Steps

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Get the cheese ready
  3. Cup of meat sauce
  4. Layer of noodles
  5. More sauce
  6. Followed by a layer of cheese
  7. Repeat until you have three layers
